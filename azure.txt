1. Geography

A Geography is a large area containing one or more Azure regions.

Examples:

India
United States
Europe



2. Region

A Region is a specific location containing one or more datacenters.

Examples:

Central India
South India
East US




3. Availability Zone An Availability Zone (AZ) is a physically separate datacenter within a region. 

Example: Region: pune, chennai, mumbai
